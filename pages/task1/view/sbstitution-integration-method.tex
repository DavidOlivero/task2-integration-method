\textbf{Temática 1 - Método integración por sustitución}
\[\int \frac{2z}{z^2-2}dz\]
\[\int \frac{2z}{u}\]
\[Siendo \ u=\constu\]
\[du=2zdz\]
\[\int \frac{du}{u}=\log \left|u\right|+c\]
\[\int \frac{du}{u}=\log \left|\constu\right|+c\]
